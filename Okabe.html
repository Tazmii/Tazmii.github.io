Okabe Rintaro (Character Model)

Okabe Rintaro by Tazmi on Sketchfab

Model of the protagonist of Steins;Gate Okabe Rintaro. The lack of 3D models of characters from this show was the main inspiration for creating this model. It was one of my first attempts at doing anime-inspired models, so a lot of experimentation was done to try to make it as accurate to the source material as possible.
