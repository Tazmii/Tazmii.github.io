Peppino (Character Model)

Peppino by Tazmi on Sketchfab

An adaptation of the character Peppino Spahghetti from the game Pizza Tower. This model makes use of bendy bones to give a cartoonish bend to the character's limbs. I also implemented a complex facial rig which allows for the squash and stretch of Peppino's eyes, mouth, nose, and head.
